About Me

Trenton Findley is a Registered Representative of NY Life Securities, LLC and licensed Agent of New York Life Insurance Company in the states of Kentucky and Indiana. He is passionate about helping people create estates for themselves and learn the value of leaving a legacy. He loves being a dedicated financial professional who helps people create their best possible financial outcome.

Trenton’s a graduate of Louisville Male High School, in Louisville, KY where he lettered in football, basketball, and track & field. He lettered four years in football at the University of Tennessee at Martin and graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Business Administration. Trenton has one son, Trenton Jr, who is 13. He enjoys spending time with friends & family, reading, golfing, and traveling.

My Work

Trenton utilizes expertise developed over 10 years in the financial industry to run his practice at New York Life. As a vibrant financial professional, Trenton focuses on coordinating the financial affairs for people in the community & organizations who value performance, prudent strategies for life savings, and a personalized relationship with a financial professional.

Over the years Trenton has developed and implemented plans for clients of all backgrounds and ages. Skills include relationship management, investments, life insurance, retirement planning, wealth preservation, long-term care planning, financial analysis, and buy-sell agreements, just to name a few.

He also is a community volunteer and passionate public speaker.
